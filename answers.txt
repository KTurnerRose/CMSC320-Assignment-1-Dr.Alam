Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: A version-control system stored outside of my local system will 
protect 
work/projects from getting lost or accidentally deleted.  Git also makes 
group 
collaboration on a project easier.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how 
it might help

Answer: Journalists can use data science to investigate trends in a 
population or 
the effects of an event on a population.  Data science can also be used 
for 
reporting projections for events journalists report on such as predicted 
election 
outcomes for specific areas of a country based on voter registration data.
